Collision repair for White Plains drivers

As the county seat, White Plains packs a lot of traffic into a small footprint — the Cross Westchester Expressway (I-287), the Bronx River and Hutchinson River Parkways, plus dense downtown parking around the transit center and the mall. That mix produces exactly the damage we fix all day: low-speed parkway rear-ends, merge-lane sideswipes, and tight-garage scrapes.

Whatever the cause, the repair standard is the same: measure the structure, restore it to factory spec, recalibrate the safety sensors, and match the paint so you can't tell it happened.
