Collision repair for New Rochelle drivers

New Rochelle sits right on Long Island Sound at the busy southern edge of Westchester, and two of the region's heaviest roads cut straight through it — I-95, the New England Thruway, and the Hutchinson River Parkway. Add a downtown in the middle of a high-rise building boom, with construction trucks, delivery vans and Metro-North commuters all fighting for the same lanes, and you get exactly the damage we fix all day: I-95 merge rear-ends, parkway sideswipes, and parking-deck scrapes downtown.

Whatever the cause, the repair standard is the same: measure the structure, restore it to factory spec, recalibrate the safety sensors, and match the paint so you can't tell it happened.
